Detailed Description

Represents the amdgpu gfx chipset version, e.g., gfx90a, gfx942, gfx1103.

Note that the leading digits form a decimal number, while the last two digits for a hexadecimal number. For example: gfx942 --> major = 9, minor = 0x42 gfx90a --> major = 9, minor = 0xa gfx1103 --> major = 10, minor = 0x3
